NPV
Net Present Value (NPV) is the value of all future cash flows (positive and negative) over the entire life of an investment discounted to the present.
Compound Intrest
Compound interest is the interest imposed on a loan or deposit amount. It is the most commonly used concept in our daily existence.
PV
Present value is the current value of a future sum of money or stream of cash flows given a specified rate of return. Future cash flows are discounted at the discount rate, and the higher the discount rate, the lower the present value of the future cash flows.
